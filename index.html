About Our Owner

J. D. Charlot, Sr.

J. D. Charlot, Sr. is a distinguished churchman and consultant recognized for his strategic foresight and impeccable timing. Known as a master of ecclesiastical protocol, he executes sacred events with precision, graceful excellence, and deep-seated compassion.

With a career-long tenure in the funeral industry, Bishop Charlot provides high-level consulting services that bridge the gap between professional administration and sacred tradition, bringing a wealth of liturgical and organizational wisdom to every client he serves.

A lifelong leader and scholar, he holds a Bachelors from Texas A&M University-Commerce. Beyond his professional and ministerial life, he is a devoted husband and the father of two.

"Excellence is not an act, but a habit. We treat your legacy with the sacred trust it deserves."
